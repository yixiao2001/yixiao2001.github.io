About Me

I am a Master student at Chongqing University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hongyu Zhang.

I am interested in intelligent software engineering with a focus on NLP applications for software development, operation, and maintenance. My current research topics involve intelligent log analytics, robust and green application of LLMs in software engineering.
